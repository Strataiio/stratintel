Why most AI implementations fail
AI transformation in manufacturing is navigating complex change — it requires deep domain expertise, AI technical expertise, and the expertise to manage organisational change simultaneously.

In our experience, focusing on tech expertise alone is the real cause of failure. A system can be technically perfect and still be abandoned — because the people, the processes, and the management structure were never ready to absorb it.

The companies that win with AI don't just find the best AI vendor. They find a partner who understands their industry, their operations, and how to move their organisation through real change.
